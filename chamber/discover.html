Facts and Statics About Salt Lake City

Population in Salt Lake City

Salt Lake City is the capital of Utah and the most populous city statewide, with over 1.25 million residents.

The Great Salt Lake

The Great Salt Lake is the largest lake in North America without an outlet to the ocean resulting the high salt concentration.

Elevation

Salt Lake City has an elevation of about 4,226 feet above sea level, which is much higher than most cities in the United States. The higher elevation means that the air pressure is lower, and as a result, there is less oxygen in the air.

Temple Square

Historic Temple Square and the headquarters of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The Salt Lake Temple took 40 years to build and its located in the heart of downtown Salt Lake City.

Public Transportation

Utah Transit Authority, or UTA, provides more than 100 bus routes in and around Salt Lake, covering 1,800 square miles.
