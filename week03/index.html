Site Purpose:

The purpose of Salt Lake City Chamber of Commerce is to promote our local business while serving our community. Whether you are starting up a new business or own a business for quite sometime, we can help you grow as you engage in events within our community. We strive in promoting events that can help you connect with different business, potential clients, partners and people. Promoting your business is our priority While helping our citizens find reliable resources.
