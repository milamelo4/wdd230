Description of Target Audience

  • Who: People interested in promoting their business. Anyone looking for local events.
  • Age: 16 and up.
  • Accessability: Desktop, laptop, tablets and smart phones.

Personas

Person 1: Zineide is a small business owner. For about two decades she has devoted her life to house cleaning. While she has not promoted her services beyond word-of-mouth referrals, she wants to expand her business by acquiring more customers. She looks forward to achieving her goal. She uses her laptop, tablet and sometimes her smart phone.

Person 2: Carina, age 41, is brand new to Salt Lake City. She has recently launched a business in vinyl wrapping and is looking for assistance connecting with potential customers. Additionally, she is hoping to secure a shop to perform the vinyl wrapping. She is familiar with using a computer as well mobile phones.

Five Listed Scenarios

Scenarios that describe the visitors purpose in visiting and using the site of which the site content can provide the answers such as:

  1. How to promote your business?
  2. How to check upcoming events in the community?
  3. If someone it's interested in my services how do i contact them?
  4. Where can I find local activities?
  5. If my request post has been filled how do I remove it from the chamber site?
